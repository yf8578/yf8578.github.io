## Abstract
The hypoxia-ischemia (H-I) diseases share some common mechanisms which may help to delay the diseases’ processing. However, the shared features are still unclear due to the lack of large scale high-quality multi-omics data that specifically target the same disease, population, and tissues/cells. In this study, we developed a novel risk assessment method to analyze four H–I diseases including eclampsia/preeclampsia (PE), pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH), high-altitude polycythemia (HAPC), and ischemic stroke (IS). A combined new evaluation score was designed to integrate evaluation information from genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, and metabolomics.
