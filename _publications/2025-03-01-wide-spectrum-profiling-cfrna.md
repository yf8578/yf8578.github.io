## Abstract
This study provides a comprehensive profiling of plasma cell-free RNA (cfRNA) across a wide spectrum, evaluating its stability, detection metrics, and analytical pipelines. The research demonstrates the profound potential of cfRNA as a non-invasive, accessible biomarker for ongoing health monitoring and early disease detection.
